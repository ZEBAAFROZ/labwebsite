Welcome to FLaMe Research Lab

Frontier in Language Understanding and Neural Modeling is a hub of innovation and collaboration, committed to advancing the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP). Our team of dedicated researchers, students, and engineers work together to develop cutting-edge algorithms that enhance machine language. Our research covers a broad spectrum, from foundational work in computational linguistics and machine learning to practical applications in human language technology.
