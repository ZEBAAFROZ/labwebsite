Research Focus

At FLaMe Research Lab, our research is driven by the belief that language technology can transform the way we interact with the world. Our work spans various domains, including Mental Health, Fake News, Claims, Propaganda, and Deception, where we apply cutting-edge NLP techniques to address complex challenges. Our projects not only push the boundaries of theoretical understanding but also offer practical solutions to real-world problems.

In each project, we strive to combine rigorous research methodologies with innovative approaches, ensuring that our contributions are both impactful and relevant. Our team is dedicated to advancing the field of NLP through interdisciplinary collaboration, and we take pride in sharing our findings with the broader community.

Automatic Speech Recognition

COIL-D

The goal is to improve the existing state-of-the-art ASR systems or propose a new model for automatic speech recognition for code-mixed languages. We work on cross-lingual transfer learning, data augmentation, and neural architectures specifically designed to handle the phonological and lexical challenges of languages like Hindi-English (Hinglish).

We develop AI-driven models for healthcare — specifically for mental health counseling and learning disorders. Our systems analyze peer support conversations in online mental health communities, identify behavioral traits that foster engagement, and generate supportive responses.

The goal is to use knowledge graphs and LLMs for explaining social biases, hate speech, sarcasm, and other complex linguistic phenomena. Rather than treating NLP models as black boxes, we build systems that produce human-interpretable explanations alongside their predictions.

This project is dedicated to developing advanced models for identifying and mitigating harmful content on social media platforms. We focus on combating the spread of fake news, misinformation, propaganda, and hate speech using multimodal learning, commonsense knowledge injection, and cross-lingual transfer.

The project COIL-D is in collaboration with Bhasini which aims at promoting the creation of language resources for Human Language Technology (HLT) applications, especially for Machine Translation, creating leaderboards for MT evaluation, and establishing benchmark setups for various linguistic tools.
